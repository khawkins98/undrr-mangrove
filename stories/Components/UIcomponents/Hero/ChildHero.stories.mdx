# Mangrove Hero (Sub)

The Mangrove Hero components are cards with information and/or image.

### Overview
The Mangrove Hero component defines a card of information with some items. It is a multi-usage component that creates boxes that are usually teasing some kind of content.

#### When to use:

When a card with data, CTA link and image is to be displayed.

### Formatting

#### Default
It consists of a title, paragraph, CTA link, and/or image with colors like green, yellow, red, blue.

### Content
There are two types of Mangrove Hero components: Mangrove Hero with Image and Mangrove Hero without Image.

### Behaviors

#### States
Mangrove Hero with image:
There are two states in mangrove Heros: Default, Hover.

Default: It shows the title, paragraph, CTA link, and mangrove Hero with Image and title, paragraph, and CTA Link for mangrove Hero with Image.

Hover: In the hover state, when the mouse hovers over the mangrove Hero with Image, the image is displayed. Along with Title, Image, and CTA Link. On hover, the background color is changing to one of the predefined accent colors: red, blue, yellow (default), or green.

Mangrove Hero without image:
There are two states in mangrove Heros: Default, Hover.

Default: It shows the title, paragraph, CTA link, and mangrove Hero without Image and title, paragraph, and CTA Link.



Hover: In the hover state, when the mouse hovers over the mangrove Hero will display the title, mangrove Hero, and CTA Link. On hover, the background color is changing to the accent color of the page.

Mangrove Hero w/o Image can be presented with the Emphasized title (without description). In this content, the card will display the title and CTA link. On hover, the background color will be changed to the accent of the page.
